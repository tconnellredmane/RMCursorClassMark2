Prediction, Not Reasoning

  • Next-Token Prediction — "What word is most likely to come next," not "What's the correct answer."
  • No Internal Model of Your System — Just pattern-matches against what's in the prompt and its training data.
  • Confident Wrongness (Hallucinations) — Looks right but is wrong — invented libraries, hallucinated APIs, subtly broken logic.
  • Non-Deterministic — Yesterday you told me "X", now you say "Y".
